========================================
toon_climb              by Pizza Supreme
========================================

A toon styled climb map that has medium difficulty. Intended
for climbers who are looking for a modest challenge. Shortcuts
included in the map if you have the skills to complete them,
perfect for speedrunners.

Special thanks to the SkillzWorld climb server for keeping tfc
fresh with a server full of climb maps!
